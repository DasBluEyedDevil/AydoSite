Our Mission

Our Values

AydoExpress

About AydoExpress

AydoExpress is our specialized logistics subsidiary focused on rapid delivery and courier services. We provide fast, reliable transportation for high-value and time-sensitive cargo throughout the known universe.

Services

  • Express Cargo Delivery
  • Secure Courier Services
  • VIP Transport
  • Emergency Supply Runs

Fleet

Our dedicated fleet consists of specialized ships optimized for speed and efficiency, including modified Cutlass Reds, Mercury Star Runners, and Constellation Phoenix vessels.

Empyrion Industries

About Empyrion Industries

Empyrion Industries is our manufacturing and resource processing division. We specialize in mining operations, material refinement, and the production of high-quality components and equipment.

Services

  • Resource Extraction
  • Material Processing
  • Component Manufacturing
  • Equipment Maintenance

Facilities

Our operations are based in multiple locations across the Stanton system, with primary facilities on Hurston and ArcCorp, and expansion plans for the Pyro system.
